About Us

At Gyans Pump, we are committed to empowering farmers with innovative solutions to tackle the challenges of climate change and water scarcity. Our flagship product, a portable, lightweight, and affordable mobile water pump, enables farmers to secure reliable water sources for year-round irrigation, ensuring greater crop yields and improved agricultural practices.

Through the Young Africa Innovates (YAI) program, we’ve refined our product based on customer feedback, improved branding, and discovered a niche market for sustainable farming tools. Together, we’re driving change in agriculture for a better future.

Why Choose Gyan Pump

Portability

Gyan Pumps are lightweight and easy to carry, allowing farmers to move them effortlessly across fields or locations.

Affordable Solution

Cost-effective pricing makes Gyan Pumps accessible to all farmers, providing quality irrigation without breaking the bank.

Easy Maintenance

Designed with simplicity in mind, Gyan Pumps require minimal maintenance, allowing farmers to focus on their work.

Effortless Irrigation for All Your Crops

Precision and efficiency with Gyan Pumps for healthier yields.

For Maize

Maize thrives with consistent watering, especially during flowering and grain-filling stages. Gyan Pumps deliver precise water flow for furrow or drip irrigation.

For Rice

Rice requires controlled flooding for optimal growth. Gyan Pumps ensure efficient water supply, maintaining the perfect water depth for your paddy fields.

For Vegetables

Vegetables need consistent but moderate irrigation. Use Gyan Pumps for precise sprinkler or drip irrigation to keep your crops healthy.
